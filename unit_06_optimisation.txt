convexity in optimization; L is convex if the line between any two points on the graph lies above the graph ‚Üí has exactly one global minimum, gradient descent guaranteed to find it
saddle point; gradient = 0 but not a min or max ‚Äî $f(x,y) = x^2 - y^2$ has a saddle at the origin: minimum in x, maximum in y

convex sums; $L = \lambda_1 L_1 + \lambda_2 L_2 + \ldots$ with $\lambda_i \geq 0$ and $\sum\lambda_i = 1$ ‚Äî a weighted average of convex functions is still convex
heuristic optimization; trial-and-error without gradient info ‚Äî e.g. random restarts, genetic algorithms ‚Äî no guarantee of finding the optimum
convergence graphs; plot of L vs iteration number ‚Äî a good convergence plot decreases smoothly and levels off; spikes suggest too-large step size
grid search; evaluate L at every point on a regular grid ‚Üí works for 1-2 parameters; fails in high-D (needs $n^d$ evaluations)

What is the feasible set?;set of all Œ∏ that satisfy the constraints ‚Äî e.g. for a probability vector: the probability simplex (all non-negative entries summing to 1)
What is the difference between a hard constraint and a soft constraint?;hard: must be exactly satisfied (e.g. c(Œ∏)=0); soft: encoded as a penalty term added to L ‚Äî soft constraints allow tradeoffs
What is relaxation in optimisation?;convert a hard/discrete problem to a simpler continuous/unconstrained one ‚Üí e.g. treat integer variables as continuous during optimisation
What is a box constraint?;$\theta_{min} \leq \theta_i \leq \theta_{max}$ for each parameter ‚Äî the simplest type of constraint; easy to enforce by clipping
What is linear least squares?;minimise $\|A\theta - b\|^2$ ‚Äî convex quadratic, closed-form solution via pseudo-inverse: $\theta = A^+ b$
What does convex mean for an objective function?;$L(\alpha\theta_1 + (1-\alpha)\theta_2) \leq \alpha L(\theta_1) + (1-\alpha)L(\theta_2)$ ‚Äî the graph is a bowl shape, gradient descent finds the global min
What is grid search and why does it fail in high dimensions?;evaluate L at every grid point ‚Üí $n^d$ evaluations for d parameters and n values each ‚Äî for d=10, n=10 that's 10 billion evaluations
What is multiple restart?;run optimisation from many random starting points, keep the best result ‚Äî reduces chance of getting stuck in a bad local minimum
What is ant colony optimisation?;agents (ants) explore the search space and deposit "pheromone" on good paths ‚Üí population + memory metaheuristic inspired by how ants find shortest paths
What is the acceptance probability in simulated annealing?;$P = \exp(-(L_{new} - L_{old})/T)$ when $L_{new} > L_{old}$ ‚Äî worse moves are accepted with probability that decreases as T cools
What does a convergence plot show?;L(Œ∏) vs iteration number ‚Äî a well-behaved run decreases smoothly and flattens; oscillations or divergence signal a too-large step size
What are the pros and cons of random search?;pros: simple, can't get stuck in local minima, no gradient needed; cons: completely inefficient ‚Äî no learning from past evaluations
What are the pros and cons of simulated annealing?;pros: less sensitive to local minima than hill climbing, widely applicable; cons: depends heavily on temperature schedule, slow, no convergence guarantee
What are the pros and cons of genetic algorithms?;pros: handles discrete+continuous, some robustness to local minima; cons: many hyperparameters, slow, no convergence guarantee, hard to tune
